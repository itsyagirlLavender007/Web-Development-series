A practice project using Nodejs, Express and EJS. 
The user will be able to input date and their thoughts.
After submitting they will be redirected to the homepage.
The data will be stored on other page which they can visit by clicking on the "Date" that will be shown under "Here are your thougts".
They can delete their data.
Styling is minimal as focus is on the functionality of Express, Node and EJS. 
Practing and understanding interaction with between frontend and backend(server) was the main goal.
